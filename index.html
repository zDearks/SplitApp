What is a Telegram Wallet?
Integrated Wallet
The Telegram Wallet is a self-custodial crypto wallet built directly into the Telegram messaging app, allowing you to securely manage your digital assets.
Convenient Access
Access your funds anytime, anywhere through the Telegram app you already use daily, without the need for additional downloads or sign-ups.
User-Friendly
Enjoy a streamlined, intuitive interface designed to make cryptocurrency management simple and accessible for users of all experience levels.
